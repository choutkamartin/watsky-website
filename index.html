A note from George on refunding concerts

What are we, crazy? Well, we’re gonna keep re-scheduling this fucking tour until it’s safe to do it. All rescheduled shows are currently offering a 30-day refund window. To refund your ticket, call the customer service number for the ticketing company you purchased from and provide them with your purchase info. If you cannot find your original ticket email, or need to be pointed in the right direction, please email watskyrelief@gmail.com and our team will help you.

Tickets for the dropped shows in Santa Fe, San Antonio, Nashville and Des Moines will be automatically released and refunded to you. I’ll come back to you soon! ‍ If you’re a ticketholder who wants to hold onto your ticket, it’s automatically valid for the rescheduled date. Just show up and rock with us when the world gets it together.

Hip Hop

Whatever Watsky does he does with all his heart, if not more. Even as his hip hop has involved to include elements of rock, jazz, and orchestral arrangements, he has held true to his distinct blend of sometimes funny, often virtuosic, always honest wordplay.

Poetry

An alum of San Francisco's youth spoken word poetry scene, Watsky has appeared on HBO Def Poetry, Ellen, CNN, NPR, PBS, Lin-Manuel Miranda's Hamilton Mixtape, toured extensively with his live band, and amassed over 200 million YouTube views. His debut essay collection, How to Ruin Everything (Penguin / Random House), was a New York Times bestseller.

Guinness Record

In May 2020, to raise money for COVID relief, Watsky set the Guinness World Record for longest rap marathon by continuously freestyle rapping for 33 hours and 33 minutes. He responds to 'George' or 'Watsky,' his family name, and is currently busy working on something more extra than the thing he did before it.
